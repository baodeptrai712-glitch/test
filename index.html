CAPCOM

Capcom is best known for action-focused game genres, especially fighting games, survival horror, and action RPGs. The company gained worldwide recognition with franchises like Street Fighter in fighting games, Resident Evil in survival horror, and Monster Hunter in action RPGs. Capcom also specializes in stylish action and hack-and-slash games such as Devil May Cry, as well as classic arcade-style action games like Mega Man.

Valve

Valve is best known for PC-focused game genres, especially first-person shooters and online multiplayer games. The company gained recognition with titles like Half-Life and Counter-Strike in the FPS genre, as well as Dota 2 in multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA). Valve also specializes in innovative game design and digital distribution through Steam, strongly influencing modern PC gaming.
